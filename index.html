Anthony Maglio is an actor, singer, and dancer based in the San Francisco Bay Area, which is where he was born and raised. He discovered his love for the arts at the ripe age of 9, after obsessing over Michael Jackson's "Thriller" music video, which blew him away. Shortly after, Anthony started his journey in theater, performing in numerous shows throughout middle school and high school. He decided to further pursue his passion by attending Chapman University and graduated with a BFA in Theatre Performance.

Anthony enjoys creating interesting and complex characters as well as singing and dancing his heart out, showcasing his love for performing. He is currently represented by Johnson Talent Agency where he gets an opportunity to do TV, film, and commercial work which he enjoys equally to the stage. Outside of the arts, Anthony enjoys the outdoors where he loves to backpack, camp and hike. He also spends a majority of his free time on the golf course, playing the greatest game there is.
